Learn more about NIM

The Department of Technology has recently unveiled their newly developed "Neural Interface Module".

Last updated: 1 October 2035





The Neural Interface Module (NIM) is the newest advancement of today's age. Created through a partnership between the Department of Technology and Department of Health, and powered by a betavoltaic battery, this device lets citizens have greater control and automation over their personal electronic devices.

How to use it

After completing the setup process, the device is very easy to use. Simply place a digit onto the under-surface capacitive sensor on the side of the NIM. Next, concentrate and think on what you want to do on your connected device.

How it works

The complex yet compact desgin would not be possible without the department of technology.

The components of the device include:
 - Antenna
 - Battery
 - Sensor array
 - Microcontroller

How to get yours today

Login and book in a time to get your NIM installed within 5 working days.

Frequently asked questions

Q: Is it safe?
A: Yes, we have had the safety of the NIM peer-reviewed, and proven that it has no negative affect on quality of life.
